Special thanks to Former Indonesian Vice-President Jusuf Kalla (Juju) for making this site possible. Singaporeans and Malaysians very appreciate your comments reminding them they need to be grateful for the generously provided clean air!

“For 11 months, they (our neighbours) enjoyed nice air from Indonesia and they never thanked us. They have suffered because of the haze for one month and they get upset.” - Former Indonesian Vice-President Jusuf Kalla (Juju)

This website is a relaunch of the original thankyouindoforthecleanair.com website which launched in 2015 amid comments made by Juju about the haze crisis in Singapore. Sadly, the original website went dark around 2019. This relaunched version was made by Indonesian Dania Rifki in her spare time, Thank you for visiting!
